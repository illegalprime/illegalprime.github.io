On Stage at Dad's

vignette

For those that don’t see color, I’m a white, Jew-ish, Easter-European manly-man who likes looking at himself. Sound familiar?

One of the things we like to do sometimes is Improv, and a cool thing about Improv is that if you pay the theater enough then you can get on stage with minimal experience. That’s pretty cool! And Dad’s Garage (the theater) is a non-profit that is genuinely one of the coolest places I’ve had the privilege of being a part of.

Anyway I wanted to brag about myself with some pictures from my friend’s flip phone of the first night I ever went on a stage, in front of people.

To be clear I didn’t get paid, Janie like to call me a “Volunteer Comedian”.

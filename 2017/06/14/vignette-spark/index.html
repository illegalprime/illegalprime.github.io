Michael's No. 1 Dating Tips #1

vignette

This is one of my number one best dating tips! If you’ve been kissing a girl or boy and want to take it to the next level, put on some nice woolen socks and start shuffling on a nearby rug. Wait for your special someone to come home and give them a kiss. If you were disciplined enough to only touch lips, they should feel a strong static shock. That’s when you whisper:

Did you feel that spark?


— Michael
